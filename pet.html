Everything you need to know about our PET Plastic Eliminator

Overview

A solar-powered chemical reactor that uses sunlight and a small voltage to break down PET plastic waste into valuable chemicals (mainly glycolic acid) and Simultaneously produces hydrogen gas (H₂), a clean fuel, from water. Instead of burning or landfilling plastic (which causes pollution), this system upcycles plastic waste into useful products. It combines photocatalysis (light-driven reactions) and electrochemistry in one device. Solves two problems at once, i.e., plastic pollution. & clean energy demand. It is a green process, which means there is no toxic emissions and is powered by sunlight. It produces marketable chemicals and renewable fuel.

In short, this project is a future-ready solution for plastic waste management and hydrogen production, aligning with sustainability goals and circular economy principles.
